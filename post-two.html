School Library

J_ne / 30 July 2020 / library

What is your favourite place in your school?.. Classroom? Canteen? Ground? Park??.. Mine was the school library. I miss it the most. It's not just the books , shelfs, tables or the best librarian, I miss the rush in those silence.

How could one be attracted to a library?. You might love reading... or you wanted to stay alone ...or you might be a padippi (that's what we call studious, no offense)..or maybe you wanted to meet up with your lover...worst case for me. With thousands of books and thousands of students going in and out of the library, have your ever seen the scene left behind. Chairs scattered ,books here and there misplaced ..uff . Yes ,I know got to run when the bell rings. I did too.

One day we stopped. We?. Oh yeah.. Everything's better when you have a crime partner. What we did?. We stood behind cleared all the mess , arranged the books in shelf and then we ran again . Definitely. The teacher is waiting to scold us for being late. One day ,two day ,three day ....the librarian caught us before we could run to class . Oh shit ! Did we messed up? . Good news.. she's taking us to the principals room.

You don't have to do something big to be noticed. A smile in comfort , a shoulder in support and a hand in help is like light in darkness.

It's my first time in the principals room. There was a chill, it has to be the AC. Huh. There she sat, Princy, with a box of chocolates welcomed us and appreciated for our doings. Imagine what a 10 year child could have felt at that moment?. Peace. Now we are official. Kind of popular. Yah!!

Changes begins here.Everyone care about the library's image and where ready to take up our role . No more cleaning the mess . No more scolding for being late. But the chocolates..they were only for us.

Soon..

Then and there ...I fall in love with books...
